About ECOFIN

The Economic and Financial Committee (ECOFIN) was established as one of the six main committees of the United Nations General Assembly in 1945. Officially designated as the Second Committee, it addresses international economic and financial issues including sustainable development, international trade, and global economic governance.

My Role as Chair

As Chair of ECOFIN at KMCMUN 2024, I guided delegates through complex economic debates on "Addressing and Combating Recent Inflation and Mass Layoffs in Western Countries". My responsibilities included moderating debates, ensuring procedural correctness, and facilitating diplomatic negotiations.

Key MUN Terminology

  • Resolution: Formal document with decisions/recommendations
  • Position Paper: Country's stance on agenda topics (25% of score)
  • Moderated Caucus: Structured discussion on specific subtopics
  • Point of Information: Question to another delegate during speech
  • Draft Resolution: Preliminary version of resolution for debate

MUN Chair Experience

I also served as Chair for KVKSMUN, where I developed comprehensive debate procedures and marking schemes. My approach emphasizes both substantive knowledge and diplomatic decorum, ensuring productive and educational committee sessions for all delegates.
