About ECOFIN Committee

The Economic and Financial Committee (ECOFIN) was established as one of the six main committees of the United Nations General Assembly in 1945. Officially designated as the Second Committee, it addresses critical international economic and financial issues including sustainable development, international trade, macroeconomic policy, and global economic governance.

My Experience as Chair

I have served as Chair for multiple committees including ECOFIN, UNHRC, and IP at various Model UN conferences. My approach emphasizes educational value, ensuring productive and enriching committee sessions for both novice and experienced delegates through comprehensive preparation and expert guidance.

As Chair of ECOFIN at KMCMUN 2024, I guided 60+ delegates through complex economic debates on "Addressing and Combating Recent Inflation and Mass Layoffs in Western Countries". My guide covers agenda analysis, country stances, debate procedures, and marking schemes.

Key MUN Terminology & Procedures

  • Resolution: Formal document outlining committee decisions/recommendations
  • Position Paper: Country's stance on agenda topics (25% of total score)
  • Moderated Caucus: Structured discussion on specific subtopics
  • Point of Information: Question directed to another delegate during speech
  • Draft Resolution: Preliminary version for debate and amendment
  • General Speakers' List: Order of speakers for opening statements
